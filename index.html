OVERPOPULATION

Overpopulation refers to a population that is too large and
thus poses a problem. Of course, this depends on the context.
Overpopulation is a major problem in developing countries like
India. It is the chief cause for many problems such as poverty,
illetracy, child labour, child marriage, etc. Overpopulation
or overabundance occurs when a species' population becomes so
excessive that people deem it must be managed. It can result
from an increase in births (fertility rate), a decline in the
mortality rate, an imcrease in immigration, or a depletion of
resources. When overpopuation occurs, the available resources
becme too limited for the entire population to survive comfortably..
